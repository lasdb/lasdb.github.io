Pay your Annual Dues

The membership annual dues values are:

Investigator: USD 20.    Posdoc: USD 15.    Student: USD 10


If you are a new member, please also fill the Membership Application Form.


The payment can be made through Paypal.
Please try the different options:

Option 1. Use your Paypal account
Select your category and press "Pay Now". In Paypay enter your account/password and make the payment.

Option 2. Pay with credit card without an account (it might not be possible in some countries)
Select your category and press "Pay Now". In Paypay enter your email. Complete your information and the credit card. Click Checkout without creating account (this might not be available in some countries).

Option 3. We will send you an invoice to pay directly with credit card
Please send an email to lasdb.development@gmail.com. Please provide your name and category of your membership (investigator, posdoc or gradsetudent). We will send you an invoice.
