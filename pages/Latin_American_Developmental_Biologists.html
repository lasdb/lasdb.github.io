Latin American Developmental Biologists Database


"Mapping Latin American Developmental Biologists - in Latin America and Abroad"

A Directory of Developmental and Stem Cell Biology Researchers and Laboratories


We have developed an online platform to collect information on Latin American developmental biologists, research areas and animal models.

The database is designed to serve as a resource for investigators, postdocs, students, and technicians working in developmental biology and stem cell research.
This initiative will help us better understand the scope and diversity of our community and will be made available as a searchable database on the LASDB website, fostering collaboration and exchange among members.

The form is simple and takes less than 5 minutes to complete.

(*) No LASDB membership required — The form is open to any researcher based in Latin America working in developmental biology or related fields.
(*) Also open to Latin American researchers abroad — We welcome entries from Latin American scientists working internationally who maintain strong ties to the region.
