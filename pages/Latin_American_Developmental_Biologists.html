Latin American Developmental Biologist Database

The "Mapping Latin American Developmental Biologists — in Latin America and Abroad" is a Directory of Developmental and Stem Cell Biology Researchers and Laboratories.

The database is designed to serve as a resource for investigators, postdocs, students, and technicians working in developmental biology and stem cell research.
We hope this initiative will become a valuable tool to foster regional and international collaborations, increase visibility of Latin American labs, and support networking across the developmental biology community.
The searchable database interface will be publicly available in the coming months.

(*) No LASDB membership required — The form is open to any researcher based in Latin America working in developmental biology or related fields.
(*) Also open to Latin American researchers abroad — We welcome entries from Latin American scientists working internationally who maintain strong ties to the region.

The form is simple and takes less than 5 minutes to complete.
